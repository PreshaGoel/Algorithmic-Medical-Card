The Medical Card

The digitalised medical card we have generated is for grades(6-12th) This is a wholesome and efficient way for schools to enter student medical data and for parents to view it. This medical will include parameters like- ENT/ general , menstrual ,eyesight, dental and mental health. During checkups student will sit infront of a camera , this will cause the student’s profile to pop up. The profile will include information like Name, Class, Roll no., Blood type, underlining health conditions, Parents name , phone number and occupation. This can be viewed by the doctor and she can continue the checkup and fill up the respective form of the particular checkup. Then this information will be converted into pictorial form( i.e. graphs, pie charts) It will also contain remarks by the respective doctor. Parents can then go to the school websites portal and login to access this information . This will help them to analyse and be updated of their wards’ health. Through these thorough reports parents can take necessary action Health is the topmost priority. As it makes it possible for children to devote more time and concentration towards studies, sports or other extra- curricular activities.
